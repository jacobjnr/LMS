Library Rules

1. Maintain silence for a conducive reading environment.
2. No food or drinks to protect books and equipment.
3. Check out books properly at the desk before leaving.
4. Return or renew books on time.
5. Handle books and resources with care.
6. Use computers and other equipment responsibly.
7. Keep mobile phones on silent mode.
8. Do not remove any library property without proper checkout.
9. Respect library staff and other patrons.
10. Keep personal belongings secure, as the library is not responsible for lost items.
11. Children should be accompanied by adults.
12. Keep the library clean and tidy.
13. Photocopying library material is subject to copyright laws.
14. Follow all posted signs and guidelines.
15. No sleeping in the library.
16. Group study rooms must be reserved in advance.
17. Do not move furniture without permission.
18. No loud conversations or disturbances.
19. Report any damaged or missing books to staff.
20. Enjoy the resources and services respectfully.
